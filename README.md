Nourish 🌿
A personal food intelligence app that uses AI to analyse your meals from photos. Snap what you're eating, get an instant breakdown of calories and macros, and track your daily nutrition against your goals.
Features

📸 Photo analysis powered by Claude AI — just snap your meal
🤔 Clarification prompts when a photo is ambiguous
⚡ Tracks calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, sugar and sodium
🎯 Customisable daily goals for calories and key macros
📅 7-day history with per-day calorie bars
⚖️ Weight logging with lbs/kg toggle
✏️ Manual entry for when you don't have a photo
💾 Data persists locally in your browser

How to use

Open the app and tap Add Key to enter your Anthropic API key
Tap Snap a Meal, take a photo, and hit Analyse
Set your daily goals under the Goals tab
Track your progress throughout the day

Note: Data is stored in your browser's localStorage. It persists across sessions on the same device but does not sync across devices.
Built with React and the Claude API. No backend, no install — runs entirely in the browser.
